## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Visit the homepage
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign In"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a magic link
5. Click the link to sign in

### Dashboard Overview

After signing in, you'll see your dashboard with:
- **Projects List** - All your product listing projects
- **Statistics** - Total projects, images, and credits
- **Quick Actions** - Create project, create brand kit
- **Recent Activity** - Your most recently updated projects

## Creating Projects

### Step 1: Create a New Project

1. Click "New Project" button on the dashboard
2. Fill in the project details:
   - **Project Name** (required) - A name to identify this project
   - **Product Name** (required) - The name of your product
   - **Product Category** (optional) - e.g., "Electronics > Audio > Headphones"
   - **Description** (optional) - Additional details about your product
   - **Brand Kit** (optional) - Select a brand kit to apply consistent styling
3. Click "Create Project"

### Step 2: Edit Project Details

1. Open your project from the dashboard
2. Click "Edit" button
3. Update any fields
4. Click "Update Project"

### Step 3: Delete a Project

1. Open your project
2. Click "Delete" button
3. Confirm deletion
4. Note: This will delete all associated images and A+ content

## Uploading Product Images

### Upload Methods

You can upload product images in two ways:

#### Method 1: Drag & Drop
1. Go to your project detail page
2. Find the "Product Images" section
3. Drag image files onto the upload area
4. Release to upload

#### Method 2: Click to Browse
1. Click "Select Files" button in the upload area
2. Choose images from your computer
3. Files will upload automatically

### Image Requirements

- **Formats:** JPEG, PNG, or WebP
- **Maximum Size:** 10MB per image
- **Maximum Files:** 10 images per project
- **Recommended:** High-quality product photos with clear backgrounds

### Managing Images

- **View:** All uploaded images appear in a grid below the upload area
- **Delete:** Hover over an image and click the X button to delete
- **Order:** Images are displayed in upload order

## Generating Listing Images

### Available Image Types

1. **Main Image** - White background, 1000x1000px (Amazon requirement)
2. **Infographic** - Feature highlights with text and visuals
3. **Feature Highlight** - Individual feature showcase images
4. **Lifestyle** - Product in use scenarios
5. **Comparison Chart** - Product vs competitors
6. **Dimension Diagram** - Technical measurement diagrams

### How to Generate Images

1. Go to your project detail page
2. Scroll to "Generated Listing Images" section
3. Click the button for the image type you want:
   - "Generate Main Image"
   - "Generate Infographic"
   - "Generate Feature Highlight"
   - "Generate Lifestyle Image"
   - "Generate Comparison Chart"
   - "Generate Dimension Diagram"
4. Wait for generation to complete (usually 1-3 minutes)
5. Generated images will appear automatically

### Requirements

- You must have at least one product image uploaded
- You need sufficient credits (varies by image type)
- Generation happens in the background - you can continue working

## Creating A+ Content

### What is A+ Content?

A+ Content is Amazon's enhanced product description format that allows you to add rich media, comparison charts, and detailed product information.

### Generate A+ Content

1. Go to your project detail page
2. Click "Generate A+ Content" or navigate to the A+ page
3. Choose content type:
   - **Standard** - Basic A+ modules (included in most plans)
   - **Premium** - Advanced modules (requires premium plan)
4. Click "Generate"
5. Wait for generation (usually 2-5 minutes)

### Edit A+ Modules

1. On the A+ content page, you'll see:
   - **Left Panel:** Module editor
   - **Right Panel:** Live preview
2. To edit a module:
   - Click "Edit" on any module
   - Modify headline, body text, or image descriptions
   - Click "Save"
3. To reorder modules:
   - Use the up/down arrows to move modules
   - Changes save automatically

### Export A+ Content

1. On the A+ content page
2. Click "Export A+ Content"
3. Choose format: PNG or JPG
4. Click "Export"
5. Download the ZIP file containing all module images

## Exporting for Marketplaces

### Supported Platforms

- **Amazon** - Main image: 1000x1000px, Additional: up to 8 images
- **eBay** - Main image: 1600x1600px, Additional: up to 12 images
- **Etsy** - Main image: 2000x2000px, Additional: up to 10 images
- **Shopify** - Main image: 2048x2048px, Additional: unlimited

### How to Export

1. Go to your project detail page
2. Scroll to "Export" section
3. Select a platform (Amazon, eBay, Etsy, or Shopify)
4. Click "Export for [Platform]"
5. Wait for processing (usually 30-60 seconds)
6. Click "Download ZIP" when ready
7. The ZIP file contains all images resized and formatted for that platform

### Export Contents

Each export includes:
- Properly sized images
- Correctly named files
- Platform-specific formatting
- Ready-to-upload package

## Managing Brand Kits

### What is a Brand Kit?

A brand kit defines your brand's visual identity:
- Logo
- Primary, secondary, and accent colors
- Font family

Brand kits ensure consistent styling across all generated images and A+ content.

### Create a Brand Kit

1. Go to "Brand Kits" from the dashboard
2. Click "Create Brand Kit"
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** (required) - e.g., "My Company Brand"
   - **Logo** (optional) - Upload your brand logo
   - **Primary Color** (optional) - Main brand color (hex format: #FF0000)
   - **Secondary Color** (optional) - Secondary brand color
   - **Accent Color** (optional) - Accent color for highlights
   - **Font Family** (optional) - e.g., "Arial, sans-serif"
4. Click "Create Brand Kit"

### Set Default Brand Kit

1. Go to your brand kit detail page
2. Click "Set as Default"
3. This brand kit will be used for new projects by default

### Apply Brand Kit to Project

1. When creating or editing a project
2. Select a brand kit from the dropdown
3. All generated images will use this brand kit's styling

## Billing & Subscriptions

### View Your Subscription

1. Go to "Billing" from the dashboard
2. View your current plan, status, and credit balance

### Subscription Plans

- **Starter** ($29/month) - 10 credits/month, basic features
- **Professional** ($79/month) - 50 credits/month, all features
- **Agency** ($299/month) - Unlimited credits, API access

### Upgrade Plan

1. Go to "Pricing" page
2. Click "Subscribe" on your desired plan
3. Complete checkout via Stripe
4. Credits are added immediately

### Purchase Additional Credits

1. Go to "Billing" page
2. Scroll to "Purchase Credits" section
3. Select a credit package
4. Complete checkout
5. Credits are added to your account

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to "Billing" page
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period
5. You can resume anytime before the period ends

### View Credit History

1. Go to "Billing" page
2. Scroll to "Credit History" section
3. View all credit transactions:
   - Credits added (subscription, purchase)
   - Credits used (image generation, A+ content)

## Tips & Best Practices

### Product Images
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1000x1000px)
- Remove backgrounds for best results
- Include multiple angles
- Show product in use

### A+ Content
- Keep headlines concise and benefit-focused
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
- Include 4-6 modules for best results
- Review and edit AI-generated content

### Brand Consistency
- Create brand kits for each brand you manage
- Use consistent colors across all listings
- Set default brand kit for efficiency

### Credits Management
- Monitor your credit balance regularly
- Plan image generation to avoid running out
- Upgrade plan if you frequently run low

## Troubleshooting

### Images Not Uploading
- Check file size (must be under 10MB)
- Verify file format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- Ensure you have an active internet connection

### Generation Failed
- Check your credit balance
- Verify you have product images uploaded
- Try again - occasional failures are normal

### Export Not Working
- Ensure you have generated images
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different platform
