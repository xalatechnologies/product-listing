## Brand Name Selection Criteria

### Must Have:
- ✅ Easy to remember
- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Available .com or .ai domain
- ✅ SEO-friendly
- ✅ No trademark conflicts

### Nice to Have:
- ✅ Short (1-2 words)
- ✅ AI-focused suffix (.ai domain)
- ✅ Action-oriented
- ✅ Memorable and brandable

---

## Recommended Selection Process

1. **Check domain availability** for top 5 choices
2. **Trademark search** (USPTO, EUIPO)
3. **Social media handle availability** (@brandname)
4. **Test with target audience** (Amazon sellers)
5. **Final selection** based on:
   - Domain cost
   - Brand clarity
   - SEO potential
   - Memorability
